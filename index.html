PREMIUM RIVERVIEW FULL HOOKUP SITES

WATER | ELECTRIC | SEWER

Our premium riverview sites are situated on a bank raised thirty feet above the river for excellent views, access, and tranquility. All sites are spacious and level back-in RV sites that are hard-surfaced. This is truly first-class camping!

Each sites has wifi, water, 50/30/20 amp hookups, and sewer.

Clean washrooms and restrooms available nearby.

COVERED BOAT SLIP

DOCK | CLEANING STATION | ICE

Our fully equiped dock has extra long slips catering to trout boats, two access ramps, and a deck perfect for casting. One slip is included with your RV lot.

Each slip is covered and has power outlets.

An ice machine and cleaning station are located on the dock for your convenience.

ABOUT

Trout Haven provides the peasure of a natural Ozark setting without leaving the beaten path. Forget about traveling miles of back roads to reach your riverside home. Our cozy RV park is just off the highway and right on the banks of the Little Red River, a record holding trout fishing destination. Trout Haven and the Heber Springs area have everything you need for a stay in your RV, including a covered dock for your trout boat. With a focus on long term guests, you can truly make us your home away from home.
